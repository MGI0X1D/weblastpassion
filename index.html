ABOUT US

As a 100% black owned and managed company, Last Passion Clothing is committed to the Broad Based Black Economic Empowerment (BBBEE) and strives to address the challenges of unemployment and skills development.

Last Passion Clothing's diversity is a fundamental component of our business ideal. We continually partner with other companies and individuals with expertise and produce world-class work to the benefit of our clients.
